Week 3

Day 12 | 2 August 2023


Today has been a fruitful day where the team has managed to accomplish a lot. Much work has been done to ensure that we design a product that is of quality and we are proud to say that we have finalised our poster and Figma prototype. The poster has been submitted this morning and the Figma prototype is ready for the exhibition tomorrow. The team will continue to refine the static website to ensure that it will be ready for tomorrow’s exhibition as well.

This will be the team’s last dailies and we will end off with a mantra that we concur with; “Good design is obvious, great design is transparent” by Joe Sparano. The team is proud of the work we have accomplished the past 3 weeks and have high expectations for tomorrow’s exhibition and will look forward to meeting everyone there.
