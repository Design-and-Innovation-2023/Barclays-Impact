Week 3

Day 11 | 1 August 2023


In preparation for the exhibition for Thursday, the team is working hard to ensure that we showcase a product that we are proud of. We are still currently in the phase of adding the final touches, in our Figma prototype and will finalise it by tonight.

One of the screens that we are working with is:

What we are trying to do is that, based on the client’s feedback yesterday, we want to incorporate auto-selection of the interest based on the user’s spendings. What we have come out with is that, the interests will be auto selected and the user can then deselect or select the interest to tailor their experience. With this, we are trying to add interactivity to the buttons, where tapping it will make it turn active (blue) and tapping it again will turn it inactive (white). The challenge the team is facing is that we are unsure how to add this interactivity inside Figma and also how to add an info message telling users that the fields have been pre-selected based on their interest and will figure it out by tonight.

Concurrently, we are also adding our reflections to the static website, which you can view it here.

We are also working on our poster for this Thursday and this is our progress so far:

The poster aims to introduce the problem statement of our product and the solution we have come out with to achieve this, with the relevant screens from the application that achieves this. At the bottom right will contain a QR code which will link users to both our Figma prototype to try it out and also our static website. We will also continue working on the poster to ensure that the poster will showcase our product elegantly.
